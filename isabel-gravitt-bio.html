The Story of Isabel Gravitt: Age, Height, Career Highlights and Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Isabel Gravitt is a well-known American actress, model, television personality and social media influencer.

She was born in Whittier, California.

Isabel is well-known in the entertainment sector for her outstanding performances.

The actress who doubles up as a model made her acting debut in 2013 with a short film dubbed, House Call.

Isabel Gravitt age

Isabel was born on August 19, 2006, making her 19 years old as of May 2023.

How tall is Isabel Gravitt?

According to Wiki of Celebs, the actress is 5 feet and 2 inches tall, and weighs about 50 Kgs.

Isabel Gravitt career

Isabel began to work in the film industry at the age of 7 years old.

In 2013, she portrayed the role of Louise in her first short film named House call.

Following the show, she got an opportunity to play the role of Alice McCarthy in the TV series American Housewife.

Later, she worked on Reality TV projects such as Walk the Prank (2016-2018) for Networks: Disney XD and Disney Channel.

Isabel also performed the role of Young Lucy in the adventure film, In Search of Fellini.

In 2018, she showed her excellent acting talent in the thriller movie, Cucuy: The Boogeyman.

She is known for Little Fires Everywhere in the role of April Jarvis.

Later, she got a chance to play the lead role of Ellie Brannock in the Netflix series The watcher created by Ryan Murphy and Ian Brennan.

How much Isabel Gravitt worth?

Reliable sources show that her net worth is between $500k and $650k as of May 2023.
