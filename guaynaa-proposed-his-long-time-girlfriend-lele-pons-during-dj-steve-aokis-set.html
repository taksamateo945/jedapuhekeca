Guaynaa Proposed His Long Time Girlfriend Lele Pons During DJ Steve Aokis Set

Publish date: 2024-08-04

On July 31, YouTuber and online sensation Lele Pons proposed to her longtime lover Guaynaa. Lele also revealed on social media that her boyfriend, who is now her fiancée, proposed to her on Tomorrowland’s main stage. During DJ Steve Aoki’s performance, Guaynaa proposed to her. While the pair has been together since 2020, they did not originally disclose their relationship. However, suspicions about them dating began to circulate when they were seen together for Se Te Nota. The pair is now engaged and planning to marry.

Lele Pons engaged longtime lover and rapper Guaynaa.

Lele Pons announced the news on Instagram, making it social media official, with a photo of the lover on his knees proposing on stage. Sharing the photos, she stated:

“We’re tying the knot!!!” Guaynaa, I adore you with all my heart!! I’m so excited to spend the rest of my life with you! 100x YESS!!!”

Lele posted photos of the enormous diamond ring, as well as other moments from the proposal and of the happy couple, in the post. The pair can be seen embracing and rejoicing in a video uploaded by the social media sensation. Paris Hilton, who was also there during the proposal, tweeted another photo from the occasion. Paris expressed her joy and congratulated her buddy, saying:

“Sis! What a fantastic occasion! How romantic to be proposed to on Tomorrowland’s Main Stage! What a vibe! LelePons and Guaynaa, congratulations! In every sense, you two are great together! I adore you and am overjoyed to have been there for this tremendously meaningful life event. You are deserving of all the love in the world. “I’m really excited about the wedding!”

Who is Guaynaa? Details concerning Lele Pons’ fiancee

Guaynaa, aka Jean Carlos Santiago Perez, is a Puerto Rican rapper and musician best known for his popular songs such as ReBoTa. In April 2019, the popular Latin song debuted on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs list. The 29-year-old star was born in 1992 and is signed to Universal Music Latino and Republic Records. The vocalist has worked on Taxi with Mariah Angeliq and Se Te Nota with Lele Pons.

In February 2020, he was nominated for a Premios Heat award with Paloma Mami and Cami. Premios Heat is the Latin Music Awards’ yearly event. Lele Pons’ fiance’s stage name is derived from the term “Guaynabito,” which is slang for an upper middle class person from the Guaynabo town. Guaynaa dated Jacky Fontanez in 2019 before starting a relationship with Lele. Apart from being a singer and rapper, Guaynaa is also well-known on social media, where he has over 6 million Instagram followers and 3 million YouTube subscribers. He seems to be active on social media and keeps his fans up to speed on any projects he is working on.
