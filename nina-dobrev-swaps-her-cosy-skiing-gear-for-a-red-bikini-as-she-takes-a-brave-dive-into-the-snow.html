Nina Dobrev swaps her cosy skiing gear for a red bikini as she takes a brave dive into the snow

Publish date: 2024-06-01

She’s been enjoying her time at California‘s Mammoth Mountain with her snowboarder boyfriend Shaun White. 

And Nina Dobrev, 32, braved the cold as she swapped her ski gear for swimwear to make snow angels at the ski resort on Friday. 

The actress could be seen in a hilarious video as she dived into the snow in a silver padded jacket before appearing in a red bikini.
