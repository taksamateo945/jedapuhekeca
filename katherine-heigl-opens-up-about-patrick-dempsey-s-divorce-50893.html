&quot;It's Really Sad&quot;Katherine Heigl Opens Up About Patrick Dempseys Divorce

Publish date: 2024-05-01

Katherine Heigl and Patrick Dempsey haven’t worked together in nearly five years, but that isn’t stopping the actress from voicing her opinion on her former costar’s recent divorce.

The blonde beauty — who starred on ‘Grey’s Anatomy’ with Patrick — said, “It’s really sad. I think it’s always hard to have to go through something like that so publicly.”

Patrick, 49, and his wife, Jillian Fink, announced they were separating after 15 years of marriage on Jan. 23. The couple have three children — daughter Talula, 12, and twins Sullivan and Darby, 7 — together.

Patrick and Jillian in March 2011.

“[The split] was a long time coming,” a source close to the couple told ‘People.’ “They were never going to make it work, but they tried.”

Katherine with Josh in September 2014.
