Nadia Nakai just bagged a really cool partnership with alcohol brand Hunter’s. It is the perfect time of the year to partner up with an alcohol brand as peeps are about to get festive.

Taking to social media to make the awesome announcement, Nadia shared that she has partnered with Hunter’s and is the face of their brand-new flavour, Red Apple.

Nadia herself says the new flavour is awesome and we are sure it is going to fly off the shelves during the festive season. Sis unleashed her saucy side to promote this brand and looks all kinds of fire.

Bagging partnerships like this are where it is at and Nadia is on it.

“ANNOUNCEMENT I'm excited to announce my brand partnership with @hunterscider new variant Hunters Red Apple, guys it tastes so YUM NO CAP! & because I love my peps WINNING 2! Watch out for my next post! We got a HUGE COMPETITION! Red apple #Huntersredappleslaps #huntersrefreshes”

Nadia has been given many awesome partnership deals in her time. For being the young fresh face that she is, Nadia has done the most to grow her career and she isn’t even close to the finish line yet.

Honey, keep doing your thing, Mzansi is so proud!

We previously reported that Nadia Nakai has taken to social media to show love to Gemini Major and Tshego. The rapper and both Gemini Major and Tshego have a solid working relationship. They have all worked with Cassper Nyovest.

The rapper took to Twitter on Monday, 26 October to give a shout-out to both the musicians. Gemini, Tshego and Nadia have worked on a couple of songs together since their music careers took off a few years back. They even make cameo appearances in the others' videos.

The 40 Bars hitmaker has shared how much she loves both the artists. The stunner said she met Gemini and Tshego through work but they have now become family to her.
