Draya Michele, Basketball Wives Star, Engaged to Orlando Scandrick

Publish date: 2024-05-18

Time to celebrate! Basketball Wives L.A. star Draya Michele is engaged to Orlando Scandrick, Us Weekly can confirm.

The Dallas Cowboys cornerback, 28, popped the question with a stunning 7-carat diamond engagement ring with a cushion cut stone.

"It was a pleasure designing this ring with Orlando. He was one of the most detailed oriented clients," Jason Arasheben, CEO of Jason of Beverly Hills, said in a statement. "He wanted to make sure the ring was perfect and she couldn't be happier."

This will be the first marriage for Michele, 30, and her husband-to-be. "Yesterday, the love of my life asked me to be his wife…I said YES!!!" the reality star captioned an Instagram pic of her bling on Friday, June 19. "Love you @oscandrick32 forever starts now."

Michele is already mom to a 12-year-old son from a previous relationship. Scandrick is also dad to 5-year-old twin girls. The couple briefly split two months ago due to hectic schedules.
