Kourtney Kardashian's Pregnancy Style: See 9 Months of Fashion Photos

Publish date: 2024-07-28

It’s been a long and stylish nine months for Kourtney Kardashian!

Since Us Weekly exclusively broke the news that the eldest Kardashian sister, 35, was pregnant with her third child (a boy!), in early June, she’s been putting her hippie-chic sense of style to work.

While sisters Kim and Khloe may grab headlines for their skin-flashing styles, classic Kourtney loves her looser, ’70s looks—all the easier for a seamless transition to maternity clothes.

So in honor of Kourtney’s nearing due date, we’re taking a look back at her chic pregnancy style—with one very special assist from Kim’s closet. Scroll down to see it all!

Month 1, April

During the first month of her pregnancy, Kourtney fearlessly worked a bold leaf-print jumpsuit and sandals for a meet-and-greet at the sisters’ Kardashian Khaos store in Las Vegas.

Month 2, May

Parisian chic! Leading up to Kim and Kanye West‘s Florentine nuptials, the ladies toured Paris for one last jaunt. That’s when the mom to Mason, 4, and Penelope, 2, was spotted in a cool-girl moto jacket, maxi Valentino dress, and heels combo.

Month 3, June

Purrrrr-fect! With her baby bump just starting to peek through, Kourtney—joined by Kris and Kendall Jenner—stepped out in SoHo wearing a leopard minidress bundled beneath a sweater robe. Still working those strappy heels, though! 

Month 4, July

Bumpin’ in the summertime! Alongside Kim and Khloe, Kourtney supported the troops at a Babies “R” Us and Operation Shower event for military moms-to-be. Scott Disick‘s longtime love channeled the season here in another jumpsuit, this time in a bright floral print.

Month 5, August

Jumpsuit mania! Five months along, the reality star really flaunted her bump’s curvature at the Women’s Health-hosted Party Under the Stars in the Hamptons, in yet another one-piece. This one, however, was quite unique for the expectant mom as it was super-femme, boasting short sleeves and white lace from head to toe.

Month 6, September

Bohemian mama! Kourtney kept it casual in a cozy all-black ensemble while strolling around Los Angeles.

Month 7, October

With just three months to go, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, out with Disick in L.A., rocked a Coachella-worthy embroidered peasant dress, teamed with a caramel-colored hat and booties.

Month 8, November

So chic—and no wonder why! Kourtney was spotted heading into her Breakfast at Tiffany’s-themed baby shower at the Beverly Hills Hotel in a black tuxedo dress coupled with patent oxfords. Does the dress look familiar? That’s because she borrowed it from Kim, who wore it while pregnant with daughter North West, now 17 months.
