Biography

Zach McGowan was born May 5, 1981 in New York City, NY. Actor best known for his TV roles including Charles Vane on Black Sails and Jody on Shameless. He began starring as Ray Sherman in LA's Finest in 2019. His feature film credits include, among others, Terminator Redemption, Snapshot and Untold Dracula. On Black Sails he and Clara Paget were playing shipmates aboard the Ranger. He was the captain of the football and ice hockey teams at the Fieldston School of Ethical Culture, which he attended through his senior year from kindergarten. Later, he studied at Carleton College, Minnesota, Northfield.
