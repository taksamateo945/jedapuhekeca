Khloe Kardashian And True Thompson Twin In Burberry Bathing Suits Is She Hiding A Baby Bump?

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Khloe Kardashian and her two-year-old daughter True Thompson had a wonderful time vacationing with Kylie Jenner as they celebrated her 23rd birthday. The sisters continue to share beautiful photos of the scenery, the tranquil water, their daughters, and of course — their swimwear. Kylie shared multiple photo slideshows as she showed off her fabulous figure while wearing several hot pink bikinis and Khloe posted sweet photos of herself and True wearing matching Burberry swimsuits. Fans were surprised to see Tristan Thompson travel with Khloe Kardashian on Kylie’s luxurious birthday vacation but it has been confirmed that the couple is back together and moving forward. True looked adorable in a matching, Burberry one-piece that was perfect for the stylish toddler. She wore a pair of khaki-colored sandals.

Khloe protected her eyes from the sun with a pair of dark sunglasses and carried an oversized, hot-pink tote.
