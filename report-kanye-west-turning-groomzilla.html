Report: Kanye West Turning into Groomzilla

Publish date: 2024-06-22

An interesting day or reporting when it comes to celebrity weddings. CelebToob has reported that Leighton Meester and Adam Brody were secretly wed this past weekend. Prepare yourself for the exact opposite.

According to RadarOnline, Kanye West is going WAY over-the-top planning his upcoming wedding to Kim Kardashian. The report has indicated that his demands are getting “out-of-control” enough that even Kim Kardashian as to curb his requests. A friend close to the source has said,

“It’s just insane. Kanye has basically billed it as the breaking the boundaries of what to expect when it comes to a wedding. This will be the event of the year. Think nothing you’ve seen before.”

According to the report, West hopes to include fashion icones Anna Wintour, Givenchy designer Riccardo Tisci and Karl Lagerfeld as well. Even Kim thinks he is getting a little out of control. As of now, the wedding is planned to be held during the Summer in France, most likely at the Palace of Versailles.

It’s certainly going to draw its fair share of media attention.
